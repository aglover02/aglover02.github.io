Assignment 7 - Summary

Session 7 Security Activity 1: Learning about Password Managers: For this activity I learned about password managers, software designed to securely store and manage passwords, alleviating password fatigue and promoting strong password practices. Password managers generate and store complex passwords, offer multi-factor authentication, and integrate with browsers. Despite the benefits, it has vulnerabilities, like master password compromise and blocking on certain websites. Understanding their history, from Password Safe to browser integration, I see their crucial role in enhancing cybersecurity. I aim to implement a password manager to streamline password management, bolster security, and advocate for their adoption to safeguard sensitive information in both personal and professional environments. Activity 2: Web Browser Safety Practice In this activity, I explored ensuring the security of my web browser by reviewing browser options and implementing safety measures. I learned to regularly update my browser and add-ons, disable unnecessary add-ons, and clear browser caches, history, and cookies to mitigate security risks. Additionally, I explored managing autoform settings to safeguard personal information and verified website certificates for secure browsing. Through private or incognito browsing, I observed how websites couldn't recognize me, enhancing privacy. In my current and future environments, I'll apply these practices regularly to protect sensitive data, maintain privacy, and safeguard against potential cyber threats while browsing the web.
