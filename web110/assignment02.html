Assignment 2 - Summary

Session 2 Internet Fundamentals/Web Browsers Activity 1: Configure browser extensions and add-ons. In this activity, I learned about browser extensions and add-ons. These can be used for a variety of tasks, such as Grammarly for grammar and spelling corrections, text-to-speech for better comprehension, and ad blockers to reduce ads. In this activity, I will be able to apply browser extensions for myself in my school and work environment by adding extensions to my laptop that will help me be more productive, like Grammarly and text-to-speech. Activity 2: Configure browser settings In this activity, I learned about and explored my browser settings. Browser settings are extremely important for things like privacy settings, security settings, password management, and accessibility/appearance. In this activity, I will be able to manage my browser settings for myself in my school and work environment by making sure that my browser is accessible for me by changing the text size and zoom levels, and by making sure my security and privacy settings are to my preference.
